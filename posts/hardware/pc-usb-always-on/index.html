PC USB always on

2020-05-25 :: andy
#hardware  #usb 

TL;DR

  • to switch usb power off when shutting pc down: enable ErP setting in bios to S4+S5.

All my usb devices were still on when my pc was shut down

I bought a new microphone which had an led on it. When the pc was shut down, the light was still on, annoying more than anything else.

I tried the windows 10 power options to no avail. After googling I found some people mentioning a bios setting S5…

After trying a few different settings, the one that worked for me was

  • ErP: Enable(S4+S5)
